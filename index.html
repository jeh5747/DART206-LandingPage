Biography

I am a Digital Multimedia Design (DMD) undergraduate student at Penn State World Campus. I played the sousaphone in the Penn State Blue Band and basketball and hockey pep bands from the fall of 2016 to the fall of 2019. I started attending Penn State in the fall of 2016 as a Mechanical Engineering pre-major. After discovering my dislike of physics, I switched to Applied Data Science in the summer of 2018. After I realized my passion was digital art (instead of data analysis), I switched to DMD in the fall of 2020.
